AWAKE Connect - Simple User Guide

A Student Sponsorship Platform by Akhuwat
How the Platform Works - Step by Step

What is AWAKE Connect?

AWAKE Connect is an online platform that helps Pakistani students get financial support for their education from generous donors around the world. It’s like a bridge that connects students who need help with people who want to help them succeed.

Simple Goal: Make education accessible for deserving students by connecting them with caring sponsors.

The Complete Journey - How It All Works

📝 Step 1: Student Applies for Help

What the Student Does:

  1. Creates Account - Student visits the website and signs up with their email and password

  2. Gets Welcome Email - Student receives a friendly welcome email explaining next steps

  3. Fills Application Form - Student provides information about:

    • Their university and studies
    • How much money they need for education
    • Their family’s financial situation
    • Their academic grades and goals

  4. Uploads Documents - Student uploads important papers:

    • Identity card (CNIC)
    • Parent’s identity card
    • School certificates and transcripts
    • University enrollment proof
    • Bills showing family expenses

  5. Creates Profile - Student adds:

    • A professional photo of themselves
    • A short video introducing themselves (1-2 minutes)
    • Information about their dreams and career goals

  6. Submits Application - Student clicks “Submit” and waits for review

What Happens: Student receives confirmation email that their application was received successfully.

🏢 Step 2: Akhuwat Admin Reviews Application

What the Admin Does:

  1. Gets Notification - Admin receives an email: “New student application received”

  2. Logs into Dashboard - Admin opens their control panel on the website

  3. Reviews Application - Admin checks:

    • Is all information complete?
    • Are all documents uploaded?
    • Does the student qualify for help?

  4. Assigns Case Worker - Admin chooses a local case worker (field officer) to verify the student’s information in person

What Happens: Case worker receives an email with their login details and assignment.

🕵️ Step 3: Case Worker Investigates

What the Case Worker Does:

  1. Gets Assignment Email - Case worker receives notification about new student to review

  2. Logs into Dashboard - Case worker opens their work panel

  3. Studies Documents - Reviews all papers student uploaded

  4. Plans Home Visit - Contacts student’s family to arrange a visit

  5. Visits Student’s Home - Goes to student’s house to:

    • Meet the family in person
    • Verify their living conditions
    • Check if financial information is accurate
    • Confirm the student really needs help

  6. Writes Report - Case worker creates detailed notes about:

    • Family’s actual financial situation
    • Student’s character and dedication
    • Whether they recommend approval or rejection
    • Any concerns or additional information needed

  7. Submits Recommendation - Case worker sends their decision back to admin

What Happens: Admin receives email that field verification is complete.

👨‍💼 Step 4: Admin Makes Final Decision

What the Admin Does:

  1. Reviews Case Worker Report - Admin reads the field investigation results
  2. Schedules Interview (if needed) - For important cases, admin arranges interview with board members
  3. Makes Final Decision - Admin decides:
    • ✅ APPROVED - Student gets help
    • ❌ REJECTED - Student doesn’t qualify
    • 🔄 NEEDS MORE INFO - Ask for additional documents

What Happens:

  • If Approved: Student receives congratulations email and becomes available for sponsorship
  • If Rejected: Student receives polite explanation email
  • If More Info Needed: Student gets email asking for specific documents

🌟 Step 5: Student Becomes Available for Sponsorship

What Happens When Approved:

  1. Student Profile Goes Live - Student’s information appears on the public website
  2. Student Gets Notification - Email says “Congratulations! You’re now eligible for sponsorship”
  3. Student Waits - Student can log in to check if anyone has shown interest

💝 Step 6: Donor Finds and Chooses Student

What the Donor Does:

  1. Visits Website - Donor browses students who need help

  2. Creates Account - Donor registers with their information

  3. Browses Student Profiles - Looks at different students:

    • Reads their stories
    • Watches introduction videos
    • Checks their academic performance
    • Reviews how much money they need

  4. Selects Student - Donor chooses which student they want to help

  5. Makes Payment - Donor pays using credit card:

    • Can pay full amount at once
    • Or set up monthly payments
    • Gets secure payment processing

  6. Receives Confirmation - Donor gets email receipt and tax documents

What Happens: Both student and donor receive emails that sponsorship is confirmed!

🤝 Step 7: Sponsorship Begins

What Both Student and Donor Get:

  1. Direct Communication - They can send messages to each other through the platform

  2. Progress Updates - Student regularly shares:

    • Academic results
    • Photos from university
    • Updates about their studies
    • Thank you messages

  3. Admin Oversight - Akhuwat monitors communication to ensure everything goes well

💰 Step 8: Money Gets to Student

How Student Receives Funds:

  1. Admin Processes Payment - Akhuwat receives donor’s money
  2. Verification - Admin confirms student is enrolled and attending classes
  3. Money Transfer - Funds are sent directly to student or their university
  4. Receipt Confirmation - Student confirms they received the money
  5. Thank You Process - Student sends thank you message to donor

📊 Step 9: Ongoing Relationship

What Continues Throughout Studies:

  1. Regular Check-ins - Student updates progress every semester
  2. Photo Sharing - Student shares pictures from university life
  3. Grade Reports - Student uploads academic results
  4. Donor Satisfaction - Donor sees impact of their generosity
  5. Admin Monitoring - Akhuwat ensures everything runs smoothly
