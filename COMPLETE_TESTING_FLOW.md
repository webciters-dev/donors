# AWAKE Connect - Complete Testing Flow

## **STEP 1: Student Registration**
- Name, Email, Password, Gender, Personal Intro
- Upload Profile Photo
- Create Account

## **STEP 2: Student - Education Details**
- University, Degree Level, Field, Program, CGPA
- Continue to Financial Details

## **STEP 3: Student - Financial Details**
- University Fee, Living Expenses
- Scholarship Amount
- Required Amount (auto-calculated)
- Continue to Profile

## **STEP 4: Student - Complete Profile**
- Career Goals, Family Info, Parents' Occupation, Monthly Income
- Academic Achievements, Community Involvement
- Social Media Links (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, WhatsApp, Twitter, TikTok)
- Upload Introduction Video (60-90 seconds, max 25MB)

## **STEP 5: Student - Upload Documents**
- CNIC, Guardian CNIC, Fee Invoice
- SSC Result, HSSC Result
- Income Certificate, Utility Bill
- Profile must be 100% complete before submission

## **STEP 6: Admin - Review & Assign**
- Login: test+60@webciters.com / Admin@123
- Go to "Applications"
- Find student application
- Review all details
- Assign Case Workers to student

## **STEP 7: Case Worker - Verify Student**
- Receive assignment
- Conduct field verification (verify family income, assess character, take notes)
- Submit verification report

## **STEP 8: Admin - Schedule Board Interview**
- Set interview date, time, meeting link
- Notify board members and student

## **STEP 9: Board - Conduct Interview**
- Board members interview student
- Board votes to approve or reject

## **STEP 10: Admin - Final Decision**
- **Approve** → Student becomes ACTIVE → Appears in marketplace
- **Reject** → Application denied

## **STEP 11: Donor - Browse & Sponsor**
- Login as donor
- Browse marketplace (search approved students)
- View student profile (photo, video, education, required amount)
- Click "Sponsor"
- Select payment plan (one-time, monthly, quarterly, bi-annually)
- Complete payment

## **STEP 12: Student & Donor - Connected**
- Donor views sponsorship status
- Student and donor can communicate
